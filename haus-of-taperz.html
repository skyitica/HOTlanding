The shop

Cut and finish, without the noise.

We built Haus of Taperz for guests who want a serious haircut in a relaxed room: clear timing, steady hands, and barbers who care how you walk out the door.

Walk-ins welcome when capacity allows. When you book online, you lock the chair and the barber who fits your schedule. Kid cuts and beard work stay in the rotation right alongside men's cuts and color.

If you prefer Spanish at the mirror, tell us when you arrive. We are happy to keep the whole visit comfortable in either language.
